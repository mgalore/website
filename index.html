The Power of OSINT

The Digital Battlefield's Most Powerful Weapon

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) is the art of gathering intelligence from publicly available sources. In today's hyper-connected world, OSINT has become the most devastating reconnaissance tool in both offensive and defensive security operations.

The Attacker's Arsenal

Sophisticated threat actors leverage OSINT to map out entire corporate infrastructures, identify key personnel, and expose vulnerable attack surfaces before launching devastating campaigns.

Colonial Pipeline Attack

Attackers used OSINT techniques to identify forgotten VPN credentials posted on the dark web, leading to a ransomware attack that crippled fuel supply across the Eastern U.S. and resulted in a $4.4 million ransom payment.

The Defender's Edge

Security teams harness OSINT to detect threat actors surveilling their organizations, monitor exposed credentials, identify impersonation attempts, and track emerging vulnerabilities before they're exploited.

Lazarus Group Exposure

Cybersecurity researchers used OSINT to track North Korean state hackers across multiple campaigns by identifying patterns in their infrastructure, payment wallets, and code reuse—resulting in the exposure of over $500M in cryptocurrency theft operations.

OSINT in Action: SolarWinds Investigation

When one of the most sophisticated supply chain attacks in history struck, OSINT techniques proved crucial in uncovering the operation's full scope. By analyzing domain registrations, SSL certificates, IP addresses, and code repositories, investigators reconstructed the attackers' infrastructure, timeline, and targeting methodology.

This public-source investigation helped identify over 18,000 potential victims and trace the attack back to its origin—revealing a nation-state campaign that had remained undetected for over 14 months.
